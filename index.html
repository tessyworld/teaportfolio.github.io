My name is Teodora Avramova and I graduated from the Bicocca State University of Milan in 2023
with a Bachelor's degree of Psychological Sciences and Techniques.
I am an outgoing and active person that loves to learn new things. In my free time, I enjoy crafting and making a jewelry with recycled materials, and hanging out with friends or family. I find it very enjoyable to overcome challenges and solve problems.

All my life, I've been told that I am a very analytical person. I like to do research and sift through data in order to discover new things. While in University, I took a Statistical class, and it had us utilizing datasets and creating visuals to figure out what the data was telling us. It was one of my favorite classes,
and really got me thinking about careers in this field. Also, Programming languages have always been fascinating to me and I wanted to challenge myself to learn at least one and I'm actually studying HTML, CSS, Java and Python for programming.

I would like to pursue a career path in Data Analytics as this is what I'm passionate about. I am very dedicated to my work and driven. When I work on a new project, I like to see it through until the end. I love visualizing data so that I can discover hidden insights and trends, and create simple visuals that easily tell the story to management, clients, or stakeholders. I am very adaptable, and I know I can provide beneficial work to any company. Life is too short to not challenge yourself and become your absolute best.
